About Us!

My name is Penny, and I am the owner of Delicious Sweets. This little shop is in Lincoln, NE. We make cupcakes to order, or you can stop by the shop and pick up a few. Our flavors are out of this world delicious. Our location is close to town, and we have a staff of 11. Our staff are college kids working toward their degrees and enjoying a small business type atmosphere while earning money to further their education expenses.
